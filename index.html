Born in Mexico. Refined through ten years of experimentation.

Since 2016, Origen Kombucha has been on a journey of fermentation and discovery. What began in Mexico has evolved into a meticulous craft, perfected over a decade of brewing in small batches.

We believe in the power of natural fermentation. Every bottle is a testament to traditional, artisan processes—using high quality ingredients and the time-honored art of brewing kombucha. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Our kombucha is naturally low in sugar, rich in living cultures, and designed to create a balanced, refreshing drink that nourishes both body and mind.
